KWALE COUNTY TRADE REVOLVING FUND(KCoTReF)


The Kwale County Trade Revolving Fund (KCoTReF) was established vide a County Assembly Act – previously The Kwale County Trade Revolving Fund Act, 2014 which was recently repealed by The Kwale County Trade Revolving Fund Act 2020 – published by the Government printer on 10th February 2021.

This system provides superior client experience, improved decision making, data accuracy and eliminate data fragmentation.

This page provides login screen where you have to input a valid user name and password. This only applies if you are registered trader.

If you are new, click on the register link. This is where you will put in your details.

In case you forget your password, this page also helps you in resetting your password.
